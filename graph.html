Double click to add vertices. Click on a vertex to select it. While a vertex is selected, clicking on another vertex will create an edge between the two vertices. Double clicking on an edge will create a break there. Double clicking on an intersection will layer it.

Pressing 'd' will delete the currently selected object. Pressing 'c' will clear the canvas. Pressing 'u' will undo the last action.

Pressing 'i' will toggle the size of the edge breaks. Pressing 'n' will toggle the vertex labels.
